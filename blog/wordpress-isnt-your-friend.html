Why WordPress Isn't Your Friend Anymore

A simpler, faster way to build your business website. In 2026, static sites + Claude AI beat WordPress on speed, cost, and security.

Shayne Shayne's Designs
09 April 2026 Published
6 min read Reading time

TL;DR

In 2026, most small businesses are overpaying for clunky, slow websites. By moving from WordPress to a "lean" setup (using tools like GitHub and Cloudflare) and using Claude AI as your editor, you can make your site 10x faster, much safer, and almost free to run. It is easier to update than WordPress because you just talk to the AI to make changes.

Key Takeaways
  • Static websites are 10x faster than WordPress and cost near zero to host
  • Claude AI makes updates easy: just talk to it instead of fiddling with plugins
  • Static sites have no database, so hackers have nothing to attack
  • Static sites get a speed boost that helps you rank higher on Google

The Problem with the "Old Way"

Imagine you bought a giant, heavy truck just to carry a single backpack to school every day. That is what using WordPress is like for most small businesses. WordPress was built over twenty years ago. It is "heavy" because it uses old technology that requires a lot of power and constant fixing.

Most business owners are stuck in "Maintenance Hell." You have to pay $20 to $50 every month just for a place to park your website (hosting). Then, you have to pay for special "plugins" to keep hackers out or to make the site load faster. Before you know it, you are spending $800 a year on a website that is still slow and frustrating to use.

In 2026, Google and AI search engines (like ChatGPT or Perplexity) care about one thing: Speed. If your site takes more than two seconds to load, people leave, and Google stops showing your site to others. WordPress sites are often like old, dusty library books—hard to search through and slow to open.

What is a "Static" Website?

When you use WordPress, every time someone clicks on your site, a computer has to "build" the page from scratch using a big database. This takes time.

A "Static" website is different. It is like having a stack of flyers already printed and sitting on the counter. When someone wants one, you just hand it to them. There is no waiting. We use professional tools called GitHub and Cloudflare to do this.

Why this is a "No-Brainer" for your Business

Super Speed: Because the pages are already "printed" (static), they load instantly. Fast sites get more customers.

Unbeatable Security: Hackers usually break into websites by attacking the database. Static sites don't have a database. It's like trying to rob a house that has no doors or windows—there is simply no way in.

Save Your Money: You can host these sites for free or for just a few dollars. You stop paying the "WordPress Tax" and keep that money in your pocket.

The "Undo" Button: Everything on your site is backed up on GitHub. If you make a mistake and delete something important, we can hit "undo" and bring it back in seconds.

Claude: Your New Website Assistant

You might worry, "If I don't have the WordPress dashboard, how do I change my text or add a photo?"

The answer is Claude. Instead of clicking through confusing menus and buttons, you just talk to the AI. You can say: "Claude, please change my office hours to 9 AM to 5 PM and add a new paragraph about our summer sale."

Claude writes the code for you. It ensures the code is clean, which keeps your site fast.

Writing Content People Actually Want to Read

Google's newest rules say that websites should be written for humans first, not robots. They look for something called E-E-A-T. This stands for:

Experience: Have you actually done this work?
Expertise: Do you know what you're talking about?
Authoritativeness: Do others see you as a leader?
Trustworthiness: Is your information honest and safe?

We use Claude to help you organize your thoughts and make sure you are answering the exact questions your customers are typing into Google. We don't just "spam" keywords; we provide real help.

Will Moving My Site Hurt My SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is just a fancy way of saying "helping people find you on Google." Some people think WordPress is better for SEO. That isn't true. Google likes sites that are fast, easy to read on a phone, and full of good information.

By moving to a static site, you actually get a "speed boost" that can help you rank higher than your competitors who are still stuck on slow WordPress sites.

Is This Right for Everyone?

This new way is perfect for about 90% of small businesses. If you are a plumber, a lawyer, a local shop, or a consultant, this is the best move you can make.

The only time it might not work is if you are building something massive like Amazon or Facebook. But for most of us, "Simple and Fast" wins every time.

The Big Picture

The internet is changing. People don't want to dig through messy, slow websites anymore. They want quick answers from businesses they can trust.

When you move away from WordPress, you aren't just changing your website software. You are choosing to be more professional, more secure, and much more efficient. You stop wasting money on "tech chores" and start spending your time on your actual customers.

The "WordPress Tax" is a choice. You don't have to pay it anymore.

Bottom Line

By switching to a modern "static" site and using Claude AI to help you write, you save money and get more customers.
