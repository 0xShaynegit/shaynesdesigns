Who I Am

I'm a web designer and developer with a passion for building fast, beautiful, secure websites. I specialise in premium static websites for small businesses, agencies, founders, and organisations that want their digital presence to reflect their real quality.

Based in Chiang Mai, Thailand and working with clients globally. I speak English fluently and am available for full-time project partnerships, ongoing support, or discovery consultations.

I moved away from heavy frameworks and "solutions" that burden websites with unnecessary complexity. My work is built with clean HTML, CSS, and minimal JavaScript—code that's fast, maintainable, and owned entirely by you.

Experience

Over the past decade, I've designed and developed websites for startups, agencies, service businesses, and established brands across Australia, Thailand, the USA, and the UK. I've worked on brand identity, product strategy, WordPress migrations, performance optimisation, and full-stack digital product development.

What I've Learned

  • WordPress sites accumulate technical debt faster than value. Simpler is almost always better.
  • The fastest website is one that doesn't ship unnecessary code. Every byte matters.
  • Trust is built through speed, clarity, and honesty—not marketing language or fabricated claims.
  • A website is not a one-time project. The best results come from ongoing partnership and refinement.

My Approach

I don't believe in templates, frameworks, or one-size-fits-all solutions. Every project starts with real discovery: understanding your business, your audience, your constraints, and your goals.

The Work

Strategy and positioning come first. Then design. Then code. The final website is built by hand—no page builders, no plugins, no proprietary systems. You get pure, readable, optimised code that you own completely.

Collaboration

You're involved at every step. I explain my thinking, show you the work in progress, and welcome your feedback. There are no surprises at the end. After launch, I provide a full handover guide and remain available for support, updates, and ongoing improvements.

Honesty First

I don't guarantee rankings, sales, or 100% uptime. Those outcomes depend on many factors beyond my control—your content, your audience, your traffic sources, your competitive landscape, and your business model. What I do guarantee is that your website will be built with precision, tested thoroughly, and optimised for real performance.
