## Key Learnings (Applying to Future Projects)

### 1. Claims Must Be Substantiated
Before publishing ANY numerical claim or absolute statement: "Can I prove this?"
- Soft language ("reduces attacks") > guarantees ("100% secure")
- Risk-reduction > superlatives
- Specificity beats superlatives

**Example:** Instead of "fastest sites in town," say "optimized for under 2.5s load on 4G"

### 2. Decision-Support Content Drives Conversions
Guides address objections better than testimonials:
- Cost guide (pricing, hidden costs, ROI)
- Timeline guide (realistic phases, what affects speed)
- Process guide (post-purchase experience)
- Decision-support guide (when service is right for them)

**Result:** Prospects self-qualify, fewer tire-kickers, higher conversion rate.

### 3. Multi-Channel Trust Building Required
Different buyers need different trust signals:
- About page → founder credibility
- Privacy/Terms → legal protection
- Blog → expertise
- Case studies → real results
- Testimonials → social proof
- Service pages → clarity

**Don't rely on single trust signal.**

### 4. Sitemap Is Critical (Not Optional)
New pages don't get indexed without sitemap update.

**Rule:** Every time you add a page:
1. Update sitemap.xml immediately
2. Add unique meta description
3. Create internal links from related pages
4. Submit to Google Search Console

### 5. Long-Form Blog (1,500–2,500 words) Ranks Better
- Generic 500-word posts don't match search intent
- Specific metrics/examples beat generalizations
- Addressing objections drives qualified traffic

**Example:** "Real Cost of WordPress Plugins" (2,000 words with cost breakdown) outranks "Why WordPress is Expensive" (500 words)

### 6. Documentation Prevents Deployment Disasters
70+ item pre-deployment checklist catches issues before launch.

**Small oversights cascade:** Forgot mobile test → post-launch bug → angry client → refund request.

### 7. Honest Messaging Builds More Trust Than Hype
Educated buyers (your market) spot marketing tricks.
- "Removes friction" > "30-40% more sales"
- "Typically 90+" > "Instant 90+"
- Limitations build trust: "Don't guarantee rankings or sales"
